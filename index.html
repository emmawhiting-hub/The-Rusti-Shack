🐢 About The Rusti Shack

Born from a love of the ocean and the magical waters of Apo Island, Philippines — we're here to gear you up for the adventure of a lifetime.

🌊

Apo Island, Philippines

Apo Island is a world-famous marine sanctuary off the coast of Negros Oriental. Its coral reefs are home to hundreds of fish species, sea turtles, and vibrant marine life.

🐢

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to experience the magic of the ocean. We provide quality gear for rent and purchase so you can snorkel, surf, and explore without worry.

🤿

Gear Rentals

Visiting Apo Island? Don't haul all your gear — rent from us! We have snorkel sets, fins, wetsuits, surfboards, and more available for daily rental right on the island.

✈️

Ships Worldwide

Can't visit us in person? No problem. We ship all of our products worldwide so you can bring a piece of Apo Island home with you wherever you are.

🪸

Reef Conservation

We're proud supporters of Apo Island's marine sanctuary. A portion of every sale goes toward reef conservation and sustainable ocean practices.

🏖️

Local & Proud

The Rusti Shack is a small, locally owned business rooted in the community of Apo Island. We work with local guides, instructors, and artisans whenever possible.

Our Story

The Rusti Shack started as a simple dream: a little beach shack where travelers could grab their snorkel gear and jump straight into the crystal-blue waters of Apo Island. What began as a one-person rental stand on the beach grew into a full gear shop serving visitors from around the world.

Apo Island is one of the Philippines' most treasured diving destinations — and we want to keep it that way. We're committed to eco-friendly practices, supporting local conservation efforts, and helping our guests connect with the ocean responsibly.

Whether you're a first-time snorkeler, a seasoned diver, or a surfer chasing the perfect wave, The Rusti Shack has everything you need. Welcome to the shack. 🐢
