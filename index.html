About The Rusti Shack

Born from a love of the ocean and the crystal-clear waters of Apo Island — we are here to gear you up for the adventure of a lifetime.

Our Story

The Rusti Shack started as a simple dream: a little beach shack where travelers could grab their snorkel gear and jump straight into the crystal-blue waters of Apo Island. What began as a one-person rental stand grew into a full gear shop serving visitors from around the world.

Apo Island is one of the Philippines' most treasured diving destinations — and we want to keep it that way. We are committed to eco-friendly practices, supporting local conservation efforts, and helping our guests connect with the ocean responsibly.

Whether you are a first-time snorkeler, a seasoned diver, or a surfer chasing the perfect wave, The Rusti Shack has everything you need.
