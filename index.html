A Living Marine Sanctuary

Apo Island is a tiny volcanic island off the coast of Negros Oriental — one of the Philippines' most celebrated dive destinations and home to a thriving, protected coral reef ecosystem.

The island has no cars, no resorts, and no crowds. Just clear blue water, warm sand, and an ocean full of life waiting to be explored.

Our Story

About The Rusti Shack

A small beach rental business born on the shores of Apo Island — and built, one snorkel at a time, into something much more.

Rusti — Founder & Owner

Meet Rusti

The Woman Behind the Shack

"I started The Rusti Shack shortly after finishing my bachelor's in entrepreneurship at BYU-Hawaii. I found a small, rusty shack on the beach that I could afford, bought it, and named the business The Rusti Shack as a play on words — where I embedded my own name into the name of my business."

Rusti launched the business in 2021 from that little shack right on the beach of Apo Island, about four miles offshore from the Negros Oriental mainland — only accessible by boat. From day one, she rented beach balls, snorkels, masks, fins, swim gear, and a surfboard or two to the steady stream of divers and tourists making the crossing from the mainland.

"Within a year, demand outgrew the shack, and I moved into a proper storefront across the street from the beach. Later, I added a small online channel for repeat customers who wanted to buy gear after they returned home."

Our Journey

How It All Started

2021

The Rusty Shack

Rusti graduates from BYU-Hawaii with a degree in entrepreneurship, finds a small, affordable shack on the beach of Apo Island, and opens The Rusti Shack — renting snorkels, masks, fins, swim gear, and surfboards to visiting divers and tourists.

2022

A Proper Storefront

Demand grows faster than the shack can hold. Rusti moves into a real storefront across the street from the beach, expanding the product range and welcoming even more visitors to Apo Island.

Online

Ships Worldwide

Repeat customers — divers from Japan, surfers from Australia, snorkelers from Germany — start asking to buy gear after going home. Rusti adds an online channel and now ships to eight countries around the world.

Reef First

Apo Island's marine sanctuary is what makes this place special. Rusti is committed to operating sustainably and hopes to give back to reef conservation as the business grows.

Locally Rooted

The Rusti Shack is an independent, owner-run business on Apo Island. Rusti works with local guides, boat operators, and the island community every day.

Gear for Everyone

First-time snorkeler or seasoned diver — Rusti believes everyone deserves to experience the magic of Apo Island's ocean. Quality gear, fair prices, no fuss.
